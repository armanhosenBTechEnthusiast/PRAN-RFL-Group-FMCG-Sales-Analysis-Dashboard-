# 📊 PRAN RFL FMCG Sales Analysis Dashboard 2022–2025

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes **PRAN RFL Group's** FMCG sales performance from **2022 to 2025** using **Power BI**. It provides deep insights into total sales, profit, cost, regional distribution, product category performance, and distributor-level analysis across Bangladesh.

---

## 📈 Key Features of Dashboard
- 💰 Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Cost & Profit Margin — KPI Summary Cards
- 📦 Total Quantity sold across all years
- 📉 Total Sales by Quarter — trend line chart (2022–2025)
- 🛒 Total Sales by Product Category — bar chart (Snacks, Beverage, Others, Dairy)
- 🏆 Total Profit by Top 5 Product Name — horizontal bar chart
- 🔵 Total Sales by Channel — donut chart (Retail vs Wholesale)
- 🗺️ Total Sales by Region — geographic map across Bangladesh
- 📋 Distributor-wise Sales & Profit — detailed table
- 📆 Year filter buttons (2022, 2023, 2024, 2025) and category slicer

---

## 🧠 Insights Derived
- **Total Sales:** ৳21,318.61M | **Total Profit:** ৳5,860.64M | **Total Cost:** ৳15,457.97M
- **Total Quantity:** 82.17M units | **Profit Margin:** 0.27 (27%)
- **Q2** recorded the highest quarterly sales at **৳5,437M**
- **Snacks** is the top-selling category at **৳5,456M**, followed by Beverage and Others
- **Chips** leads profit among all products at **৳515M**, followed by Detergent (৳496M)
- **Retail** channel slightly dominates at **50.56%** vs Wholesale at **49.44%**
- **Tariq Enterprises** is the highest-grossing distributor with ৳1,085,643,718 in sales
- Sales are spread across all major divisions: Dhaka, Chattogram, Rajshahi, Khulna, Barishal, Sylhet, Rangpur
